To get to the hotel "Novotel":

 -- From the International Airport (Pulkovo-2 ): Take the bus #13 or the 
    shuttle  bus to the "Moskovskaya" metro station. Take metro line 2, 
    direction "Prospekt Prosvestcheniya" then change the train at "Nevskiy
    Prospekt",  take the metro line 1, direction "Rybatskoe", 
    and get off at "Mayakovskaya/Ploschad  Vosstanija" (one  stop). 
    Exit to Mayakovskogo street and cross Nevskiy prospekt.
    The hotel entrance is close to the corner of Nevskiy and Mayakovskogo street. 
    From the International Airport you may also take shuttle bus  or minibus
    (route taxi), the cost of the trip to "Moskovskaya" metro may vary 
    from 20 to 30 roubles. You may use service of private companies (EUR 50-60). 
    There is a reliable taxi  service in the International airport. The 
    dispatcher wearing a badge with blue  writing "TAXI" stands near the 
    entrance (usually from outside). It costs about EUR 50-60 to get to the hotel 
    "Novotel".  
    NB: we do not advise to accept offers of unauthorized drivers!

 -- From the railway station "Moskovskiy Vokzal": walk along Nevskiy Prospekt
    to Mayakovskogo street (about 5 min).

 -- From the railway station "Vitebskiy Vokzal": Take the metro
    ("Pushkinskaya"), line 1, direction  "Lesnaya", get off at "Ploschad 
    Vosstanija" (two stops). Exit to Ploschad Vosstanija, then walk along 
    Nevskiy Prospekt to Mayakovskogo street (about 5 min).

 -- From the railway station "Varshavskiy Vokzal": Take the metro
    ("Baltiyskaya"), line 1, direction  "Lesnaya", get off at "Ploschad 
    Vosstanija" (four stops). Exit to Ploschad Vosstanija, then walk along 
    Nevskiy Prospekt to Mayakovskogo street (about 5 min).
