IFAC Workshops ALCOSP 2007 and PSYCO 2007 Organizing Committee would like to offer tiered sponsorship opportunities and exhibit space for interested corporations. The opportunity for industry sponsorship is to access an assembled audience of industrialists, researchers, educators and students specialized in modeling, control, identification, learning and intelligent systems, filtering and signal processing. Other aspects are marketing, recruitment, and a window onto newly developing cutting edge techniques, technologies, and applications in the important areas of process applications, design, and management.

Any sponsoring company may select a number of items listed below as a part of their sponsorship package. If you are interested in being a sponsor or exhibitor, please contact the Chair, Prof. Alexander Fradkov or the Exhibits Chair Prof. Boris Andrievsky at alcosp07@physcon.ru.

No Sponsorship Package * Sponsorship fee (euros)
1 Company name and logo printed on the Workshop-bag, on the Workshop Proceedings (CD-ROM), logo inside of Program Guide, stating company as a sponsor; free brochure insert in workshop registration pack; one complimentary workshop registration and two places at the workshop banquet. 2000,00
2 One exhibit booth (8'x20'), one complimentary workshop registrations and two places at the workshop banquet 1500,00
3 One page color advertisement in Workshop Proceedings (CD-ROM), two complimentary workshop registrations and four places at the workshop banquet. 1000,00
4 One page black/white advertisement in Program Guide 600,00
5 One page company description in Program Guide 600,00
6 Free brochure insert in workshop registration pack 600,00
* Customized sponsorship package may be offered upon request. Please, contact the Exhibits Chair Prof. Boris Andrievsky at alcosp07@physcon.ru

The deadline for application to sponsorship is 15th May, 2007.
