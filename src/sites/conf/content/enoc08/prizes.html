ENOC Young Scientist Prizes 2008

Two ENOC Young Scientist Prizes 2008 will be attributed at the end of the Conference. These prizes, 250 euro each, will reward the best presentations given during ENOC-2008 by scientists less than 35 years old at the time of the Conference.

To apply for these prizes, young contributors must send an electronic mail to the ENOC-2008 Conference Secretariat (enoc08@physcon.ru) before 15 November December, 2008. This e-mail must specify the date of birth of the applicant, his or her address and the title and number of his or her papers.

Note that applicants must be the presenting author of their contribution.
