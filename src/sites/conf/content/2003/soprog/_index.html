Saint Petersburg - former Russian capital city - offers a lot of attractions to visitors. Various guided tours over St.Petersburg and suburbs including visits to Tsars' palaces and museums will be organized.
Symposium banquet will take place on August, 22 in one of historical buildings of Saint Petersburg.
Since a lot of tourists and guests are expected in the year 2003 in Saint Petersburg due to the 300th anniversary of the city, you are recommended to make advance booking of your tours, see Registration form .

All further information concerning social program will be posted on the PhysCon 2003 website: http://physcon.ru
