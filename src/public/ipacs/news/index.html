Jan. 2012. 'Cybernetics And Physics' (CAP) journal

19 Aug 2013

The 1st issue of new IPACS journal 'Cybernetics And Physics' is released: http://cap.physcon.ru/. The scope of the journal includes:
* Nonlinear dynamics and control
* Complexity and self-organization
* Control of oscillations
* Control of chaos and bifurcations
* Control in thermodynamics
* Control of flows and turbulence
* Information Physics
* Cyber-physical systems
* Modeling and identification of physical systems
* Quantum information and control
* Analysis and control of complex networks
* Synchronization of systems and networks
* Control of mechanical and micromechanical systems
* Dynamics and control of plasma, beams, lasers, nanostructures
* Applications of cybernetic methods in chemistry, biology, other natural sciences
The papers in cybernetics with physical flavor as well as the papers in physics with cybernetic flavor are welcome.

10.09.2009. PhysCon 2009 is over

19 Aug 2013

The 4th International Conference on Physics and Control (Physcon 2009) held in Catania, September 1-4, 2009 is over.
The conference was a success with about 200 participants from more than 30 different countries.The organizers (University of Catania and IPACS) thank all the sponsors and the organizers of minisymposiums and special sessions who have greatly contributed to the success of the conference.
All the papers presented at the conference are included into the IPACS library. Furthermore, a selected number (about 100) of papers are collected in a volume published by the World Scientific Publisher: From Physics to Control Through an Emergent View.
Edited by Luigi Fortuna (University of Catania, Italy), Alexander Fradkov (Russian Academy of Sciences, Russia) & Mattia Frasca (University of Catania, Italy), World Scientific Series on Nonlinear Science, Series B., 2010

ENOC'08 is over

19 Jan 2009

The Sixth EUROMECH Nonlinear Dynamics Conference (ENOC 2008) was a success with 302 registered participants from 38 countries. There were 190 international participants, including 20 from Germany, 18 from Italy, 16 from Netherlands, 15 from UK, 13 from France, 12 from USA, 10 from Ukraine, 8 from Japan and Iran. 325 talks (5 plenary, 275 oral and 45 poster) of more than 370 submitted were included into the preliminary program. 284 full text papers are included into the CD-ROM Proceedings. All the papers presented at the conference are published in the IPACS Open Access Electronic Library at http://lib.physcon.ru by September, 2008. The Conference Photo Gallery is posted at http://album.physcon.ru/gallery.html?gal=enoc08. At the closing ceremony it was announced that the next ENOC Conference will be organized in 2011 in Rome, Italy.

The Second International Conference "Physics and Control" (PhysCon 2005) took place on August 24-26 in St.Petersburg, Russia

4 Sep 2005

It was a success with 190 registered participants. 46 participants came from outside of Russia, including 9 from Japan, 6 from Germany, 3 from China, Italy, Mexico, Portugal, 2 from France. 212 talks (6 plenary, 3 invited and 70 poster) representing 35 countries were included into the preliminary program. 184 papers are included into the CD-ROM Proceedings and will be published in the IEEExplore Electronic Library. It was decided to organize the next "Physics and Control" Conference in 2007, probably in Potsdam, Germany. After the closing ceremony the foundation meeting of the International Physics And Control Society (IPACS) took place. At the meeting the IPACS Regulations have been approved and the new International Society has been established. The Physics and Control Resources portal will now be conducted by the IPACS Board. More information will be available soon.

Welcome to the information web-portal "Physics and Control Resources"(PHYSCON)!

25 Apr 2004

In August 2003, the 1st International Conference "Physics and Control" was held in St.Petersburg, Russia. The conference was a success with about 250 participants from 33 countries and demonstrated existence of a broad scientific community at the borderland between Physics and Control and a strong need to enhance information exchange among its members. To promote such an exchange the information portal "Physics and Control Resources" (PHYSCON) has been launched. Visitors will find at PHYSCON information about centers and groups, events, education and information websites, organizations and funds, publications, news - everything related to Physics and Control worldwide. We invite you, your friends and colleagues to visit PHYSCON at http://physcon.ru Send your comments and/or suggestions as well as additional information and links for inclusion into PHYSCON to info@physcon.ru
